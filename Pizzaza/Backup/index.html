Piz’za-za, your restaurant

Welcome to Piz’za-za, the place to enjoy a tasty meal in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

Dine on local products and the best wines

Since 1994, Piz’za-za welcomes local products and first quality vineyard lovers. Every month, a different sommelier selects the best wines to pair with our reasonably priced daily specials

Our aim is to serve a meal that delights the senses, nourishes the body and warms the heart any time of the day. Our promise: share each time our know-how so you feel at home to enjoy a quick bite or linger over a cozy dinner.
